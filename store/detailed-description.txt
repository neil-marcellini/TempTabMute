TempTabMute lets you mute any Chrome tab for a specific amount of time. When the timer runs out, the tab unmutes itself — no need to remember to turn it back on.

HOW IT WORKS
• Click the TempTabMute icon on any tab
• Press the Mute button to silence the tab for your chosen duration
• A live countdown shows exactly when audio will return
• Need sound back sooner? Hit "Unmute Now" at any time

CUSTOMIZABLE DURATION
Open Settings right inside the popup to pick from presets (30s, 1 min, 5 min, 15 min, 30 min, 1 hour) or type any custom duration like "1h 3m", "70s", or "90" seconds.

LIGHTWEIGHT & PRIVATE
• No data collection — everything stays in your browser
• No background network requests
• Minimal permissions: only tabs (to mute/unmute), storage (to save your preference), and alarms (to schedule the unmute)

Perfect for muting radio adds, temporarily silencing a noisy tab during a meeting, a video call, or when you just need a moment of quiet.
